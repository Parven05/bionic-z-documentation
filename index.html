Game Overview : The Bionic Z

the general idea of the game

The player who is work as lumberjack force to survive in zombie apocalypse world with his son. A evil businessman behind the reason for the apocalypse happen due to sell a harmful energy drink that change humans to zombies after consume it. So the player need to murder the businessman and save the world !

Core Mechanic

what mechanics will you use ? ( world / character / enemies )

World Mechanic

  • In World, have 24 hours system which include day & night cycle .

Character Mechanic

  • The player can rotate ( 8 directions )
  • Joystick for movement
  • Primary weapon of player is axe, he can swing it and use timer for the next attack
  • Secondary weapon of player is guns, include skill cooldown. Each guns consist 2 types of shooting styles
    • First Skill: Basic attack
    • Second Skill: Ultimate attack

Enemies Mechanic

  • The zombies in default they are laying on the ground, and when they "smell the player's brain" they get up and gradually increases their speed
  • The zombies can bite player and there would be a 30% chance to get illness
  • If the player shoot the zombies they will get more aggressive and faster because of thier pain
  • The zombies will die fast, if the player shoot on head twice
