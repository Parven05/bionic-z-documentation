Game Overview : The Bionic Z

the general idea of the game

The player who is work as lumberjack force to survive in zombie apocalypse world with his son. A evil businessman behind the reason for the apocalypse happen due to sell a harmful energy drink that change humans to zombies after consume it.So the player need to murder the businessman and save the world !
